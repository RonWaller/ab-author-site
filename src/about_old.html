Like many writers, my passion for writing started at a very young age. Any chance I got to write a short story, poetry or what I called a novel back then, I took it. Writing brought me joy and a way to express myself.

As life tends to do, it got in the way of writing and was put on the back burner for a very long time. I would try to write from time to time, but it just never went anywhere.

It wasn't until a loss in the family hit me extremely hard that I was reminded life is short. We cannot let any second pass that is not lived to its fullest potential.

Writing is back in my life and will never leave again.

I currently live with my wonderful husband, Kris, and our pupper, Bella. They make my days bright and my smile brighter.
